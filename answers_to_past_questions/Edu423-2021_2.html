    1. Define Measurement

      2. Answer:

      Measurement is a broad term that refers to the systematic determination of outcomes or characteristics by means of some sort of assesment devices.

      It is a systematic process of obtaining the quantified degree to which a trait or an attribute is present in an individual or object.

      It is a systematic assignment of numerical values or figures to a trait or an attribute in a individual or object
